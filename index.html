T-EST

An automatic mold cost estimate program for injection, die-casting, rubber, and packing after 3D automatic mold design. It quickly and accurately estimates costs through 3D modeling analysis, a rich database, and know-how on estimation.

T-EST drastically reduces the time and effort involved in the initial quotation process, ensuring competitive pricing and faster response times for your clients.

T-MOLD

The solution for full 3D automatic injection and Die-casting mold design based on NX. It has a proven record of shortening design time from 20 days to 12 days and achieving 0% design defects in success cases.

T-MOLD streamlines complex mold design processes, ensuring precision and efficiency from concept to completion. It minimizes human error and accelerates your production cycle.

T-EDM

The solution for 3D automatic electrode design based on NX. All post-works are automated after the semi-automated creation of the shape section, including drawing, CAM exporting, and order sheets. Reduces design time by over 30-50%.

T-EDM optimizes the electrode design workflow, ensuring accuracy and consistency while significantly cutting down on manual labor and potential errors.

T-GUN

The solution for automatic creation of drawing, work orders, and Gun-Drill machining CAM through 3D modeling. It can recognize properties of Gundrill holes created in T-MOLD without additional settings, allowing one-click output.

T-GUN ensures precise and automated generation of necessary documentation and CAM data for gun drilling, enhancing efficiency and reducing setup times in manufacturing.

T-HOLE

The solution for automatic creation of drawing, work orders and CAM for the Hole part through 3D modeling. It enables outputting setting drawings, CAM sheets, and NC data with a single click by recognizing hole properties from T-MOLD.

T-HOLE automates the tedious process of hole feature detailing, guaranteeing accuracy and speeding up the preparation for machining operations.

MOLD-MANAGER

An easy and comfortable system for managers and workers to manage work schedules for each process from obtaining an order to shipping. Based on Web and Android, it allows data sharing and quick decision-making, increasing company productivity.

MOLD-MANAGER centralizes project information, providing real-time updates and facilitating seamless communication across all stages of mold production, from order to delivery.

T-VIEW

A powerful viewer solution for professionals, managers, and field workers who operate within the NX environment. It provides easy access and visualization of complex 3D models without needing a full license of the design software.

T-VIEW enhances collaboration by allowing stakeholders to review and comment on 3D designs efficiently, irrespective of their CAD software expertise, thus speeding up approval processes.
